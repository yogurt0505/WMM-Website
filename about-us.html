Our Store

The giant robot anime franchise Gundam is a popular culture icon in Japan. It is also were we find our passion and inspiration. We love this hobby and the community who share an interest around it.

Gakuteki is the spot where you can find all about this piece of Japanese culture in the Malaysia.

Founder of Gakuteki.my - It all started out during the year 2022. We found out that there weren't really any shop that sold Gundam Model Kits In Malaysia.

  • Hence, we have decided to get sources from local and overseas suppliers to start selling online and that's how Gakuteki.my was born.
  • When Gakuteki.com started, we sell less than 100 model kits per month, slowly thanks for the support of each Gunpla fans, we have sell more than 1000 Gundam Model Kits through Gakuteki.my.
  • Beside that, we also selling our Products on different E-commerce Platform such as Lazada, Lelong, Shopee.

Whether you are a new modeler or an expert we are here to provide you the best experience, top customer service and support so you can excel in this amazing hobby and take your craft to the next level.
